Inventory Mangment Module is for a Hospital Managment.

User Info:
It will allow the Admin of the Inventory Department to recodrd the Products as they are added and stored in the inventory.
While Admin will also be responsible for distribution of these items to the other departments and mintain regular recodrs of all transactions carried out.

Module Sections:
The module has, Dashbord, Add Items, All products, Genral items, Hygenic Items, Medical items, Electrical Items, Other Items, Orders and Report Section.
It will allow the admin to maintain the records easily.
